FAQ

Where does the data come from?

All data comes directly from Companies House — the UK's official registrar of companies. It's published under the Open Government Licence, so it's free to use and redistribute.

How fast are the alerts?

We check Companies House once per day. When a new filing is detected, alerts are sent immediately. For most use cases — insolvency monitoring, director changes, corporate events — daily checks are more than sufficient.

Is this affiliated with Companies House?

No. We're an independent service that uses the public Companies House API. We're not endorsed by or affiliated with Companies House or the UK government.

How does payment work?

Payments are handled securely via Stripe. Tap /upgrade in the bot, choose your plan, and pay with any debit or credit card. Your account is upgraded instantly after payment.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Use /cancel in the bot to downgrade to free. No contracts, no questions asked.
